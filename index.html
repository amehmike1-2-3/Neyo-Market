📖 Our Story

NeyoMarket was founded in 2026 with a single, urgent purpose: to give Nigerians a marketplace they could actually trust. The founder had seen first-hand how everyday buyers lost their hard-earned money to fake vendors, how genuine sellers went unpaid after delivering goods, and how the lack of a secure payment layer was strangling digital commerce across the country.

The answer was escrow — but built properly, into every single transaction by default, not as an optional add-on. NeyoMarket was engineered from the ground up as a full-stack escrow marketplace, powered by Paystack, where no seller ever receives payment until the buyer confirms delivery. Every vendor undergoes KYC identity verification with a valid NIN or BVN before listing a single product. Every dispute is reviewed within 24 hours with a full refund guarantee if the seller is at fault.

Beyond security, NeyoMarket was built to be fast on any Nigerian mobile network — no heavy app downloads, no 4G requirement, no barriers. Whether you're selling eBooks from Lagos, shipping fashion items from Kano, or earning affiliate commissions from Enugu, NeyoMarket is the platform that works for you — safely, fairly, and without middlemen taking more than their share.
